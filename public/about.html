About politicly.io

Politicly is a free, non-partisan platform for voters to keep track of the political campaign promises made by candidates up for election. The information on this site is submitted by users of the site, and is not endorsed by politicly in any way.

You can help us keep this information accurate. If you see anything missing, incorrect or out of date, please submit the correct information. You will need to register to submit information, but we promise not to use your personal information in any way.

To Add a Candidate

Navigate to The new candidate page. Enter the candidate details, then you will be able to start filling in that candidate's political platform.

If you have any questions, concerns, or need more information, you can reach us here.

We're In Beta

Politicly.org is in beta mode. This site was quickly put together in order to test the market for such a site. If you see any problems or have a suggestion for a new feature, please get in touch at info@politicly.org. Screen shots and detailed explanations are our friend! And thanks for checking us out! We hope you decide to stay.
