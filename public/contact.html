About politicly.io

Politicly.org is a new venture to test the market for a crowd-sourced political reference site. We're just getting up and running and are very open to hearing your suggestions or criticism. If you'd like to get in touch, the best way to reach us is at info@politicly.org.

We're In Beta

Politicly.org is in beta mode. This site was quickly put together in order to test the market for such a site. If you see any problems or have a suggestion for a new feature, please get in touch at info@politicly.org. Screen shots and detailed explanations are our friend! And thanks for checking us out! We hope you decide to stay.

Privacy

We don't share your personal information with ANYONE. We're not collecting any information aside from the username that you have provided us with. If you'd like to delete your account or any of the information that you have entered into the site, please reach out to us at info@politicly.org and we'll be happy to help.
